| Component | Formula | Industry Benchmark |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **Power Cost** | kW Ã— Hours Ã— Rate Ã— PUE | $0.08-0.15/kWh |
| **Cooling** | Included in PUE | PUE 1.2-1.8 |
| **Rack Space** | # Racks Ã— $/rack/month | $500-2,000/rack |
| **Physical Security** | Fixed + variable | 5-10% of facility |
| **Connectivity** | Bandwidth Ã— $/Mbps | $1-5/Mbps |

#### Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) Calculation

```
PUE = Total Facility Power / IT Equipment Power

Example:
- IT Load: 100 kW
- Total Facility: 150 kW
- PUE = 1.5

Annual Power Cost = IT Load Ã— 8,760 hrs Ã— Rate Ã— PUE
                  = 100 kW Ã— 8,760 Ã— $0.10 Ã— 1.5
                  = $131,400/year
```
