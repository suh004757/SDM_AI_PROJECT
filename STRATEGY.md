## 2. The Operational Engine: O.D.A.L. Loop
**"The System Runs the Loop. Humans Watch from Outside."**

Unlike traditional tools where humans are inside the loop (Human-in-the-loop), our goal is **Human-on-the-loop**.

### ðŸ”„ The Cycle
1.  **Observe**: Real-time ingestion of logs, metrics, tickets, and costs.
    *   *System Action*: `Cost_Analysis` module scans AWS billing API.
2.  **Decide**: Context-aware decision making based on historical data and business logic.
    *   *System Action*: "Cost projection exceeds budget by 15%. According to policy B, non-critical pods must be scaled down."
3.  **Act**: Autonomous execution of valid commands.
    *   *System Action*: Execute Terraform script to resize instances.
4.  **Log**: Embedding the "Reasoning" and "Outcome" into the institutional memory.
    *   *System Action*: Record *why* this decision was made into the Vector DB for future audit.

> **Human Role**: The human defines the *Policy* for the "Decide" phase and reviews the *Audit* from the "Log" phase. **They do not touch the "Act" phase.**
